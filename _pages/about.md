I am a sophomore undergraduate student in Mechatronics Engineering at [South China University of Technology](https://www.scut.edu.cn/en/) (SCUT), with a GPA of 3.9/4.0 and an IELTS score of 7.5.

I am currently a research intern at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/), working with [Prof. Hao Tang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=9zJkeEMAAAAJ) on video world models for embodied AI. My current project focuses on building multi-view action-conditioned video world models based on the LingBot-World framework, incorporating 3D spatial features (VGGT) to enhance geometric consistency. We are targeting a submission to NeurIPS 2026.

My research interest lies at the intersection of **video generation** and **robot learning**, with a particular focus on **World-Action Models (WAMs)** — systems that jointly predict future visual states and robot actions to enable generalizable embodied intelligence. I am inspired by recent works such as [DreamZero](https://dreamzero0.github.io/), [LingBot-VA](https://github.com/Robbyant/lingbot-va), and [UWM](https://weirdlabuw.github.io/uwm/).

I am actively seeking **summer research or remote internship opportunities** for 2026 in world models and embodied AI.
