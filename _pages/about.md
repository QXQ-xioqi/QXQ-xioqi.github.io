I am a sophomore undergraduate student in Mechatronics Engineering at [South China University of Technology](https://www.scut.edu.cn/en/) (SCUT).

I am currently a research intern at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/), working with [Prof. Hao Tang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=9zJkeEMAAAAJ) on multi-view video world models, targeting a submission to NeurIPS 2026.

My research interest lies in building **world models** that understand physical dynamics, and using them to enable robots to **reason, plan, and act** in the real world.

If you are working on world models, embodied AI, or video generation for robotics, I would love to connect and explore potential collaboration opportunities.
